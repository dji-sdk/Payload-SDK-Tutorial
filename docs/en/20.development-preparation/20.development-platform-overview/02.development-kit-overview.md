Its abundant computing power can be applied to flight, gimbal, and camera control, improving efficiency across various operational tasks. Developers can take advantage of its convenient development environment to create high-performance edge applications for scenarios such as energy inspection and public safety.

#### Other SDK Development Kit

* [E-Port Developer Kit](https://store.dji.com/product/dji-e-port-development-kit): includes E-port adapter board (not compatible with SkyPort V2 and X-Port). It can be used for rapid development of payload device adapted to the DJI flight platform, and can also be integrated into the finished payload as a module, providing developers with a ready-to-use development platform.

* SDK Round Ribbon Cable (**Discontinued**):  
  Developers integrate the coaxial cable into a custom onboard computer according to the pin definition, and use this cable to connect the custom onboard computer to the flight platform.  
  > Note: This product has been discontinued and is provided for historical solution reference or maintenance of existing deployments only.

* PSDK Mounting Bracket (**Discontinued**):  
  Used to mount PSDK payloads onto Matrice 30 series flight platforms, and supports quick removal and replacement of different types of PSDK payloads.  
  > Note: This product has been discontinued and is applicable to already deployed devices only.

* E-Port V2 Coaxial Cable [(Click to purchase)](https://store.dji.com/cn/product/dji-e-port-v2-coaxial-cable-kit?vid=192711): Converts the E-Port V2 port on the Matrice 400 flight platform into a coaxial cable port. Developers can design payload hardware ports according to the coaxial cable pin definitions.

* SkyPort V3 Coaxial Cable [(Click to purchase)](https://store.dji.com/cn/product/dji-skyport-v3-coaxial-cable-kit?vid=192861&from=search-result-v2&position=0&total_result=8): Used to connect the SkyPort V3 adapter ring on the Matrice 400 flight platform and convert it into a coaxial cable port. Developers can design payload devices that support quick-release gimbal ports according to the coaxial cable pin definitions.

* SDK Certification Chip [(Click to purchase)](https://store.dji.com/product/dji-sdk-certified-chip?vid=190941): Used to authenticate third-party payload devices developed based on the DJI E-Port/E-Port Lite ports. It ensures that the device can operate normally and communicate effectively with the aircraft, serving as a payload security certification and function authorization.
    > Note: The certification chips are sold as a kit (50 pcs), and the listed price applies to the entire kit. In actual development or mass production, each PSDK payload device uses one certification chip on a one-to-one basis.

## Purchase Development Board

It is recommended that developers refer to [STM3240G-EVAL(STM32F407IG)](https://www.st.com/en/evaluation-tools/stm3240g-eval. html) parameter information to select the development platform to be used.
